2. Description of Services

MassImageComp is an image compression and processing tool that provides:

  • Image compression to reduce file size
  • Image format conversion (HEIC, JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, etc.)
  • Image resolution and size adjustment
  • Batch image processing
  • Photo library analysis by size, format, or date
  • Background processing: The App can continue processing images in the background, allowing you to use other apps or lock your device while large batch operations complete. Background processing is subject to iOS system limitations and may be paused or terminated by the system based on battery level and device resources.
  • Progress tracking: The App displays a badge number on the app icon showing the remaining number of images being processed, helping you monitor progress even when the App is not in the foreground.

All image processing is performed locally on your device. No images are uploaded to any server.
