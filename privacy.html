Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MassImageComp (“we”, “us”, or “the App”) is an image compression and processing tool for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to users worldwide, except Mainland China.

By using this App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect and Permissions

We only collect and use information necessary to provide our services:

  • Photos: All image processing happens locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or analyze photo content.
  • Photo Library Permission: Used only to select images and save processed results. No data is transmitted to our servers.
  • Network Access: Used for advertisements (free version), in-app purchase validation, and update checks.
  • Notification Permission (optional): Used only for task completion reminders.

We do not actively collect personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Data

  • Anonymous device identifiers (for subscription validation via iCloud)
  • Anonymous usage statistics and crash reports
  • Subscription purchase records processed by Apple
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA, free version only)

We do not create user profiles.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Photo data remains on your device only
  • Subscription status may sync via iCloud
  • Anonymous analytics retained only as needed
  • Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer required

4. Third-Party Services
