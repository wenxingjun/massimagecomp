Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I restore purchases?

A: Please go to the "Settings" page in the app and tap "Restore Purchase". If your subscription or purchase cannot be restored, please contact us via email and provide your Apple ID email address.

Q: What are the limitations of the free version?

A: The free version can process up to 3 images per batch. Upgrade to Pro to unlock unlimited batch processing, all output formats, and ad-free experience.

Q: What image formats are supported?

A: The app supports common image formats such as JPEG, PNG, HEIC, WebP, and GIF. Pro users can unlock all output format options.

Q: Are images uploaded to the server?

A: No. All image processing is done locally on your device. We do not upload or store any of your image data. You can view our Privacy Policy for more information.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: You can manage your subscription in iOS "Settings" ‚Üí "Apple ID" ‚Üí "Subscriptions". After canceling, you can still use Pro features until the current subscription period ends.

Q: Why can't I see the lifetime version?

A: The lifetime version may be temporarily unavailable for the following reasons: the product is still being configured, your region is not supported, or the product is not linked to the current version. If you still cannot see it, please contact us.
