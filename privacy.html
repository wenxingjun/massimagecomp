Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 2025

MassImageComp (“we”, “us”, or “the App”) is an image compression and processing tool for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to users worldwide, except Mainland China.

By using this App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data to provide and improve our services:

Identifiers

  • User ID: Anonymous UUID generated locally for user identification and analytics. Not linked to your identity.
  • Device ID: Device model, operating system version, and device identifiers for app functionality, analytics, and third-party advertising (if applicable).

Purchase Information

  • Payment Information: Processed by Apple through App Store. We do not directly collect payment details.
  • Purchase History: Subscription status and purchase records for app functionality (managing subscriptions, restoring purchases).

Usage Data

  • Product Interaction: User actions such as page views, button clicks, feature usage, and image processing events for analytics and app functionality.
  • Other Usage Data: Session IDs, timestamps, app version, language, timezone, and system information for analytics and app functionality.
  • Ad Data: Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data for third-party advertising (if applicable).

Diagnostics

  • Crash Data: Crash logs, error reports, and diagnostic information for app functionality and analytics.
  • Performance Data: App performance metrics for app functionality and analytics.
  • Other Diagnostic Data: Additional diagnostic information for app functionality and analytics.

User Content

  • Photos or Videos: Selected images for compression processing. All processing happens locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or analyze photo content.
  • Email or SMS: If you use the feedback feature, we may send feedback emails. Email addresses are only collected if you provide them.
  • Customer Support: Feedback and support requests you submit through the app.

Permissions

  • Photo Library Permission: Used only to select images and save processed results. No data is transmitted to our servers.
  • Network Access: Used for advertisements (free version), in-app purchase validation, analytics, and update checks.
  • Notification Permission (optional): Used only for task completion reminders.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • Photo data: Remains on your device only. We do not store or upload photo content.
  • Subscription status: May sync via iCloud for cross-device functionality.
  • Analytics data: Stored on our servers (massimagecomp.com) and retained only as needed for service improvement.
  • Third-party data: Advertising data is handled by third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob) according to their privacy policies.
  • Data deletion: Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer required for the purposes stated in this policy.

  • Purpose: Data analysis, app improvement, and service optimization.

Apple Services

  • App Store: Payment information and purchase history are processed by Apple for in-app purchase validation.
